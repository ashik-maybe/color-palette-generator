About This Dictionary

In 1934, Sanzo Wada published "A Dictionary of Color Combinations" - a seminal work documenting traditional Japanese color harmonies.

This digital recreation honors his methodology while adapting it for the modern age. Each color combination is generated using principles from his original work.
